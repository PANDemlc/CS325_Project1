Purdue buries '23 loss, into Final Four behind Zach Edey's 40
Purdue secured a Final Four spot by defeating Tennessee 72-66, overcoming last year's upset loss. Zach Edey's exceptional performance with 40 points and stellar defense led Purdue, highlighting a redemption story for the team after last year's disappointment. Coach Painter reflected on the team's journey and celebrated the victory.
