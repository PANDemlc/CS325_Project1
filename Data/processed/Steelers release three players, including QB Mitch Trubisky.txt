Steelers release three players, including QB Mitch Trubisky
The Steelers released QB Mitch Trubisky, OL Chukwuma Okorafor, and punter Pressley Harvin to save $13M in 2024 salary cap space. With Trubisky gone and Rudolph a pending free agent, rookie Kenny Pickett is the only QB on the roster. Trubisky struggled as a backup, starting 7 games for the team.
