Chelsea's Hayes slams Arsenal boss Eidevall's 'male aggression'
Chelsea manager Emma Hayes accused Arsenal boss Jonas Eidevall of "male aggression" on the touchline during the Conti Cup final, which Arsenal won 1-0. The altercation involved Chelsea midfielder Erin Cuthbert. Hayes criticized Eidevall's behavior, while Eidevall defended himself and mentioned pre-match disagreements over the playing system.
