Chelsea's Hayes slams Arsenal boss Eidevall's 'male aggression'
Chelsea manager Emma Hayes accused Arsenal boss Jonas Eidevall of "male aggression" on the touchline during the Conti Cup final. Arsenal won 1-0 in extra time, with tensions rising between the managers. Eidevall denied the allegations and criticized Hayes' behavior post-match. Eidevall claimed the conflict began due to a disagreement over playing with one ball versus a multiball system.
