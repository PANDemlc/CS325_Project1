Shohei Ohtani optimistic about recovery after mashing HRs in BP
Shohei Ohtani, Dodgers' designated hitter, impressed during batting practice hitting 10 of 21 pitches over the fence, indicating progress in his recovery from elbow surgery. Despite not pitching this season, Ohtani signed a record-breaking contract and aims to play in the season opener against the Padres in South Korea.
